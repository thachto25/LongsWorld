Character Bio


My character's name is Long. He is an Eastern-inspired humanoid dragon. He originates from Long Island, where dragons and humanoid dragons have established themselves, where dragons and humanoid dragons have established themselves. Though they cohabit, humanoid dragons are seen as “inferior” due to their human characteristics and thus treated unfavorably. It is not uncommon for humanoid dragons like Long to move elsewhere, where they are perhaps a little more accepted, once they reach maturity.
